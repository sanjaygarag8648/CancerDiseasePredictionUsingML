What does Predict do?

Predict asks for some details about the patient and the cancer. It then uses data about the survival of similar patient in the past to show the likely proportion of such patient expected to survive up to their surgery with different treatment combinations.

Who is Predict for?

Predict is for clinicians, patients and their families. Patients should use it in consultation with a medical professional.
